This is how it works
-------------------

.. raw:: html

   <div class="step-card">
     <span class="step-number">1</span>
     <span class="step-title">Download Trézor Suite</span>
     <div class="step-content">
       <p>Download Trézor Suite for desktop for the best experience, or click "Continue in browser" to open the web app.</p>
     </div>
   </div>

   <div class="step-card">
     <span class="step-number">2</span>
     <span class="step-title">Install the app</span>
     <div class="step-content">
       <p>Install the desktop app on your computer. This step isn't necessary for web app users.</p>
     </div>
   </div>

   <div class="step-card">
     <span class="step-number">3</span>
     <span class="step-title">Set up your Trézor</span>
     <div class="step-content">
       <p>Plug in your device and follow the steps to set up your Trézor hardware wallet. This takes about 15 minutes.</p>
     </div>
   </div>

   <div class="step-card">
     <span class="step-number">4</span>
     <span class="step-title">Move your coins</span>
     <div class="step-content">
       <p>You're ready to protect your crypto securely. Check <a href="guides/transfer-coins.html">our guide</a> to learn how to transfer your assets from an exchange to your Trézor.</p>
     </div>
   </div>
